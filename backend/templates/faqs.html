Get quick answers to common questions

What types of businesses can use Kirazee?

Kirazee supports multiple business types including restaurants, grocery stores, food courts, pharmacies, and other retail businesses. Each business type gets customized features and templates suited to their industry.

How does the onboarding process work for businesses?

Businesses go through a multi-step onboarding flow:

  • Basic information (business name, type, features)
  • Address & location details (with Google Maps integration)
  • Financial details (bank account, GST)
  • Template selection
  • Verification and payment (₹999) After approval, businesses can start managing their operations.
Can I manage multiple businesses with one account?

Yes, Kirazee supports master business accounts where you can manage multiple business locations or franchises from a single dashboard with consolidated reporting.

How does the payment system work?

The platform integrates with a hosted payment gateway for business onboarding fees and consumer orders. It supports order creation with payment tracking, and includes features for wallet management and vouchers. The payment flow redirects to a secure payment page and returns users to the app after completion.

Can I manage multiple business locations or branches?

Yes, Kirazee supports master business accounts where you can manage multiple locations from one dashboard, view consolidated reports across all branches, include/exclude branch data in reports, assign staff to specific locations, and each branch can have its own inventory, suppliers, and menu while maintaining centralized oversight. The system automatically detects if you're a master business and provides appropriate multi-location features.
