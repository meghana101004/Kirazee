Fast, reliable delivery from your favorite local restaurants and grocery stores

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

At Kirazee, we partner with trusted local restaurants and grocery stores to deliver fresh meals and essentials quickly, safely, and reliably. Our mission is to make your food and grocery shopping simple, convenient, and enjoyable—right from your phone to your doorstep.

Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver across select areas based on the service range of our partner stores. Delivery availability may vary depending on your pin code and the specific restaurant or grocery partner.

Delivery Timeframes

Restaurant
Restaurant Orders: Delivered within 30–90 minutes, depending on restaurant distance and order volume.

Grocery
Grocery Orders: Typically delivered within 2–4 hours from the time your order is placed.

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by factors such as traffic, weather, or high order volumes.

Delivery Charges

  • Delivery fees depend on distance, partner store policy, and order value.
  • Free delivery may be offered for orders above the minimum value set by the store.
  • Any applicable delivery fee will appear at checkout before confirming your order.

Order Fulfillment

Each restaurant or grocery store is responsible for preparing and packaging your items. Our delivery partners ensure proper handling, especially for perishable or temperature-sensitive goods.

Tracking & Notifications

Track your order in real time through the "My Orders" section on our app or website. You'll also receive updates by SMS, email, or in-app notifications at each delivery stage.

Delivery Verification

To ensure safe and accurate delivery, our riders may request verification using an OTP or a digital signature upon handing over your order.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Order Cancellation by Customer

Restaurant
Restaurant Orders: Can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing the order. Once the restaurant begins preparing your food, cancellation is no longer possible.

Grocery
Grocery Orders: Can be cancelled before the partner store confirms your order. After confirmation or dispatch, cancellations are not allowed.

To cancel: Go to "My Orders" → select your order → tap "Cancel Order"

Order Cancellation by Store or Platform

An order may be cancelled by the store or by Kirazee in cases such as:

  • Item unavailability
  • Incorrect pricing or system error
  • Delivery address out of coverage
  • Payment or verification issues

If payment has been made, the refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Failed Deliveries

If our delivery partner is unable to reach you after multiple attempts or if the address is incorrect, the order will be marked as "Failed Delivery."

Food
Food Orders: Non-refundable once prepared.

Grocery
Grocery Orders: Partial refunds may apply depending on item condition and store policy.

Refund Timelines

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after order cancellation or return confirmation. Timelines may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
