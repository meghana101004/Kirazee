Refund Policy — Last Updated: October 2025

1. Overview

At Kirazee, we aim to ensure complete customer satisfaction with every order. However, if your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or incomplete, you may be eligible for a refund as per the following policy.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund eligibility depends on the product category and the nature of the issue.

a. Restaurant Orders (Food Delivery)

Refunds are issued only if:

  • The wrong item was delivered
  • The order was not delivered
  • The food was severely damaged or spoiled upon arrival

Once food preparation starts, orders cannot be cancelled or refunded unless the restaurant fails to deliver.

b. Grocery Orders

Refunds are available for:

  • Damaged or missing items
  • Incorrect product delivery
  • Expired or low-quality items

You must raise a refund request within 4 hours of delivery with supporting photos.

c. Pharmacy Orders

Refunds are available only for:

  • Wrong or damaged medicine delivery
  • Product with broken seal or expired item upon delivery

Prescription medicines cannot be refunded or returned once delivered, unless there is a quality or packaging issue.

d. Retail and Other Products

Refunds are accepted for manufacturing defects, incorrect items, or damages during transit. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and reported within 48 hours of delivery.

3. Refund Process

Submit a refund request through "My Orders" → select the order → "Report an Issue."

Our support team will review your request and may contact you for verification or additional details. Once approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. Refund Mode

Refunds are processed through the same payment channel used for the purchase:

  • Online Payments (UPI, Cards, Wallets): Refund credited automatically to the same account.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Refund issued via UPI, wallet credit, or coupon code after confirmation.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind or dislike of product after delivery.
  • Incorrect address or unavailable customer during delivery.
  • Orders cancelled after packaging or dispatch (except for non-delivery cases).
  • Perishable goods like food or certain medicines once delivered.

6. Partial Refunds

In case of multi-item orders, refunds may be processed partially if only certain items are affected or unavailable.

7. Refund Timelines

Instant refund may be offered as wallet credit in some cases. Bank refunds typically take 5–7 business days depending on your payment provider.

Refund Cancellation Policy

1. Customer-Initiated Refund Cancellation

You may cancel the refund request before it is processed. Once the refund has been initiated to your bank or wallet, it cannot be reversed. To cancel, contact customer support with your Order ID and Refund Request Number.

2. Platform-Initiated Refund Cancellation

Kirazee reserves the right to cancel or deny refund requests if:

  • The issue reported is invalid or unverifiable.
  • The item has been tampered with, misused, or returned in unsellable condition.
  • Refund abuse or fraudulent behavior is detected.

3. Post-Refund Cancellation

Once the refund has been processed and credited, it cannot be withdrawn or reversed. If you wish to re-purchase the product, you can place a new order on the platform.

4. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund or cancellation decision, you can raise a dispute by emailing support@kirazee.com. Our escalation team will review the case within 48 hours.
