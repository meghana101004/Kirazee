Last Updated: January 2025

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Kirazee Limited ("Kirazee", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website at kirazee.com or mobile applications. By using our platform, you agree to this policy.

Your Consent

By using Kirazee services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use our platform.

Information We Collect

Account Information: Name, email, phone number, address, password, and profile picture.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and device identifiers to improve your experience.

Usage Information: How you use our services, search terms, preferences, and interaction data.

Order & Transaction Data

We collect information about your orders, payments, delivery addresses, restaurant preferences, ratings, reviews, and transaction history to process orders and improve our services.

Location Information

We collect your location data (with your permission) to show nearby restaurants, estimate delivery times, and provide location-based services.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to process orders, provide customer support, improve our services, send notifications, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.

Data Sharing

We share your data only with restaurant partners (for order fulfillment), payment processors (for transactions), delivery partners (for delivery), and service providers (for app functionality). We never sell your personal data.

Data Security

We protect your data using SSL encryption, secure servers, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is 100% secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account data anytime. You can also opt-out of marketing communications and request data portability. Contact us for assistance.

Data Retention

We keep your account data until you delete your account, order history for 7 years (for legal compliance), and support communications for 3 years.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via email or app notifications. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to other websites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Please read their privacy policies before sharing information.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover such data, we'll delete it promptly.
